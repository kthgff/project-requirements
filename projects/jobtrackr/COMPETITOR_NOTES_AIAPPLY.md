# AIApply Reference Notes for JobTrakr

## What Keith Means by "work like AIApply"

Interpretation:
JobTrakr should not just track jobs. It should behave like an AI-powered job search copilot that helps across the full workflow:
- find or ingest jobs
- evaluate fit
- manage jobs in one workspace
- help tailor application materials
- support or automate parts of the apply flow
- improve speed from discovery to interview readiness

## Observed AIApply Product Pattern

Core areas visible from AIApply messaging:
- auto apply to matched jobs
- AI resume builder
- AI cover letter generation
- interview preparation
- resume optimization / ATS scanning
- job board / job discovery
- high-match recommendation workflow

## Product Implication for JobTrakr

JobTrakr should evolve from a narrow inbox tracker into a broader AI job search assistant.

### Core product pillars
1. Job ingestion and discovery
2. Match scoring and prioritization
3. Job management workspace
4. Application material generation
5. Application workflow support or automation
6. Interview preparation support

## What Stays True for Current MVP

The current MVP can still begin with:
- Google login
- protected dashboard
- searchable jobs table
- Gmail ingestion
- resume upload
- match percentage
- fit flagging

That remains the right foundation.

## What Changes Strategically

The long-term target is larger than simple job tracking.

JobTrakr should be planned as a platform that can later add:
- resume tailoring per job
- cover letter generation per job
- ATS optimization checks
- auto-apply flows where practical
- interview prep and coaching
- recommendation engine for best-fit jobs

## PM Recommendation

Do not expand the MVP immediately into every AIApply feature.

Instead:
- keep the current MVP foundation intact
- adjust roadmap and vision so future releases clearly expand toward the AIApply-style experience
- frame JobTrakr as an AI job search copilot, not only a job inbox tracker

## Immediate Product Direction Update

Updated positioning:
JobTrakr is an AI-powered job search copilot that ingests opportunities, prioritizes them using resume-based match intelligence, organizes them in a searchable workspace, and will expand to help tailor and automate applications.
